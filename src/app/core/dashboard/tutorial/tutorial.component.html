How does it work?

Calendar

Timeliness uses your calendar to create events marking your deadlines (indicated by the 📅 emoji) and of course specific events we call work units (indicated by the ⚙️ emoji), that block out time in your calendar, so you know when to work on which task.
These events will be created in a separate calendar, that Timeliness will create, so they won't pollute your existing calendars.
